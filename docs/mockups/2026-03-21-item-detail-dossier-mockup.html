Body
本文
OpenAI outlined a new enterprise operating model for automation agents, emphasizing auditability and approval boundaries over raw autonomy. The company described how planning, execution, and verification layers should be separated in production systems to make agent behavior reviewable and compliant with internal controls. The framework is intended for organizations moving from demos into ongoing operational usage, where questions about permissions, human oversight, and evidence retention become central to deployment decisions.
